PHYSIOTHERAPHY
  • What is physiotherapy?
     Physiotherapy is an evidence-based healthcare profession that views human movement as central to health and wellbeing.
     Physiotherapy aims to maintain, restore and improve your function after disease or injury, so you can get back to doing what you love 
     and enjoy an injury-free lifestyle that doesn’t hold you back.

  • What conditions can physiotherapy be beneficial for?
  1. Back pain
  2. Neck pain
  3. Muscle pain
  4. Joint pain
  5. Grain and Pelvic pain
  6. Headache and vertigo
  7. Sport injuries
  8. Post-operative rehabilitationPost-operative rehabilitation
  9. Neurological rehabilitation for stroke, Parkinson’s disease, spinal cord injuries and TBIS
  • Can I claim this service as an extra on my health insurance policy?
     Physiotherapy sessions may be claimed on private health insurance depending on your level of cover, 
     please check with your private health insurance provider.
  • Do you provide services for work or motor vehicle accidents?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with injuries that have arisen from a workplace or motor vehicle accident. Please give our front desk team a call to discuss your individual needs.
