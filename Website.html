Anil Department of Physiotherapy:


Anil Hospitals are home to the world-class Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Department, which offers a wide range of treatments and services to patients. Physiotherapy treatments are taken by patients to restore, maintain, and improve their mobility. Physiotherapy helps people improve their overall health, prevent and treat injuries, as well as physical rehabilitation. We have a highly skilled and council registered team of physiotherapists, occupational therapists, registered prosthetist, and orthotist. We are dedicated to our aim of helping patients reach independent functioning and full recovery.
